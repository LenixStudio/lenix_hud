# lenix_hud
HUD for PvP servers

## About
### Introduction
Welcome to Hud.

This script is designed to enhance your server's gameplay by:
* UI that let your players look to how much health and armor quality they have 

### Instructions
* Installation: drag and drop
* Environment: QB
* Support: available with priority

> Enjoy your improved gaming experience with Hud!
